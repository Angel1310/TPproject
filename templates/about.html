Ludo is a strategy board game for two to four players,
in which the players race their four tokens from start
to finish according to the rolls of a single die. Like
other cross and circle games, Ludo is derived from the Indian
game Pachisi, but simpler. The game and its variations are
popular in many countries and under various names.
